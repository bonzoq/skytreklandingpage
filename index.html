Everything a Pilot Needs

From importing your flight log to sharing it with the world — Skytrek has you covered.

Import Flights

Seamlessly import flight tracks from SkyDemon EFB with a single tap through the share sheet.

Log Details

Add photos, aircraft registration, type info, and descriptions to every flight in your logbook.

Organize Trips

Group flights into trips with aggregate stats — total distance, duration, and aerodromes visited.

Trip Maps

See all your routes on beautiful multi-color maps with airport pins and labels.

Export & Share

Create polished trip map images with custom labels and text overlays to share anywhere.

Social Feed

Browse and share trips with the pilot community. See where other aviators are flying.

Your Own Pilot Profile Page

Every Skytrek pilot gets a public profile at mypilotprofile.com — a personal aviation page you can share with anyone. No app download needed to view it.

  • Flight hours, distance, and trip stats at a glance
  • Public flights and trips with interactive maps
  • Aircraft flown and aerodromes visited
  • A shareable link that works on any device

How It Works

Three simple steps from cockpit to community.

1

Fly & Record

Use SkyDemon or your favourite EFB to record your flight track as you fly.

2

Import to Skytrek

Share your flight log directly to Skytrek. Add photos, aircraft details, and a description.

3

Share Your Adventure

Organize flights into trips, create beautiful map images, and share with the community.
